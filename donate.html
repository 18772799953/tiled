Become a Sponsor

As a company or brand, consider becoming a Tiled sponsor! Apart from contributing to Tiled development, it can also gain you publicity on Tiled's front page by linking an image of your choice to your website or product. There are currently three sponsor levels:

  • $100 / month - Link on Tiled's front page
  • $200 / month - 8 hours/month dedicated development time
  • $300 / month - Link on Tiled's front page and 8 hours/month dedicated development time

There is also the option of contracting me to add to Tiled whatever features you need!
